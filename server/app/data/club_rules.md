# Sailing Club Safety Rules
## Potomac River - KDCA Area

### General Wind Limits (Flying Scot Boats)

| Condition | Wind Speed | Gust Speed | Status |
|-----------|-----------|------------|--------|
| Safe | 0-12 knots | < 15 knots | GREEN - Go sailing |
| Caution | 12-18 knots | 15-22 knots | YELLOW - Experienced sailors only |
| Unsafe | > 18 knots | > 22 knots | RED - No sailing |

### Weather Alert Rules
- **Thunderstorm Warning**: All boats must return to shore immediately
- **Small Craft Advisory**: No recreational sailing permitted
- **Wind Advisory**: Sailing at discretion of harbor master
- **Heat Advisory**: Ensure adequate hydration; shortened sessions

### Temperature Rules
- Below 50F (10C): Cold water protocols in effect; PFDs required at all times
- Below 40F (4C): No recreational sailing

### Visibility
- Visibility below 1 mile: No sailing permitted
- Fog advisory: No sailing until advisory lifts

### Additional Safety Notes
- All sailors must check in with the harbor master before departure
- VHF radio channel 16 must be monitored at all times while on water
- Life jackets must be worn by all crew in CAUTION or higher conditions
