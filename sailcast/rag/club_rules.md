# Sailing Club Rules & Safety Guidelines

## Wind limits
- Do not sail in sustained winds above 20 knots without approval.
- Gusts above 25 knots: consider reefing or staying in.
- When in doubt, stay ashore.

## General safety
- Always wear a PFD on the water.
- Check weather before leaving the dock.
- File a float plan when going outside the harbor.

## Boat handling
- Know your boat's limits and your own.
- In heavy air, reef early.
